### $A$ -> $A\alpha_1 | A\alpha_2 |...|A\alpha_n|\beta_1|\beta_2|...|\beta_n$ 
Leads to
$A$ -> $\beta_1A'|\beta_2A'|...|\beta_nA'$
$A'$ -> $\alpha_1 A'| \alpha_2 A'|...|\alpha_n A'|\varepsilon$

leads to
$A$ -> $\alpha A'|\gamma_1|\gamma_2|..|\gamma_n$
$A'$ -> $\beta_1|\beta_2|...|\beta_n$
